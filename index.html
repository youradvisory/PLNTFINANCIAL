✱ The engagement

How we work together

Three ways to work with us, each scoped and priced before anything begins.

01

Insurance Review & Implementation

A single, scoped engagement. We measure your current cover against what your income, debts and dependants would actually need, recommend the right structure across life, TPD, trauma and income protection, then manage the application and underwriting end to end. You finish with cover you understand. No ongoing commitment.

02

Ongoing Advice

For clients who want the full picture kept current: insurance, super, investments and retirement, reviewed every year and adjusted as life and legislation change. A standing relationship, with support at claim time, rather than a once-a-year check-in.

03

Single-Issue Advice

One decision, properly advised. A redundancy payout, a business insurance structure, consolidating your super. We scope it and price it up front, so you get a clear answer without committing to anything ongoing.

No minimum balance to qualify. If advice would help you, it's available to you.
