If something hasn't gone the way it should, we want to hear about it and put it right. Here is how to raise a concern, and what happens next.

Contact James Flannery directly:

We will acknowledge your complaint promptly, look into it properly, and aim to resolve it as quickly as we can. By law, complaints must receive a final response within 30 calendar days.

If you would rather raise the matter directly with our licensee, or you are not satisfied with our response, you can contact Insight Investment Partners Pty Ltd (AFSL 368175) via www.iipdealergroup.com.au.

If your complaint is not resolved to your satisfaction, you can take it to the Australian Financial Complaints Authority (AFCA). AFCA provides a free and independent dispute resolution service for financial services complaints.
