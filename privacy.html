Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

PLNT Financial Pty Ltd ("PLNT", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, hold, use and disclose your personal information, in line with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles.

What we collect

To provide financial advice we may collect:

  • Identity and contact details: name, date of birth, address, phone, email
  • Financial information: income, expenses, assets, liabilities, superannuation, insurance and investment details
  • Employment details and family circumstances relevant to your advice
  • Health information, where required for personal insurance applications or claims (collected with your consent)
  • Government identifiers where the law requires them, such as your Tax File Number for superannuation purposes

How we collect it

We collect information directly from you: in meetings, by phone or email, and through our fact find and intake process. With your written authority, we also collect information from third parties such as your superannuation fund, insurer, accountant or employer.

Why we collect it

  • To understand your circumstances and provide personal financial advice
  • To implement and review the advice you ask us to act on
  • To meet our legal obligations, including under the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth) and anti-money laundering laws
  • To communicate with you about your plan, our services and reviews

Who we share it with

Your information may be shared, only as needed to deliver our services, with:

  • Our licensee, Insight Investment Partners Pty Ltd (AFSL 368175), for compliance and audit purposes
  • Product providers such as superannuation funds and insurers, to implement your advice
  • Service providers who support our business, such as paraplanning, software and IT providers, under confidentiality obligations
  • Your other professional advisers, such as your accountant or solicitor, with your consent
  • Regulators and other bodies where the law requires it

We do not sell your personal information. We do not routinely send personal information overseas; if a service provider stores data outside Australia, we take reasonable steps to ensure it is protected to Australian standards.

How we hold and protect it

Your information is held in secure, access-controlled systems. We take reasonable steps to protect it from misuse, interference, loss and unauthorised access, and we retain records for the periods required by law (generally at least seven years after our relationship ends).

Marketing

We may contact existing clients about services directly related to our relationship. You can opt out at any time, and every commercial electronic message we send includes an unsubscribe option. We will action unsubscribe requests within five business days.

Access and correction

You can ask for access to the personal information we hold about you, and ask us to correct it, by contacting us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable time and will not charge you for making the request.

Questions or privacy complaints

Contact James Flannery on 0478 078 825 or james@plntfinancial.com.au, or write to PO Box 39, Palmwoods QLD 4555. If you are not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner on 1300 363 992 or at www.oaic.gov.au.
